What this does

This webpage allows you to to convert a currency to another.
It requires a valid input and has a filtered list of
currencies. The first 3 letters represents the currency
you are converting from. The next 3 are for the
currency you wish to convert to. On the right, it
display in order: the amount you entered, starting
currency type, final finaly currency type, conversation
ratio at that time and final converted amount.

Purpose of this

The purpose of making this project was to increase
familiarity with Sass and Angular. Sass was used in
practice of making all style changes.Angular bindings
were used for the tabs, clicks, and list values.
